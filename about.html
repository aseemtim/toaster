About Mr. Toast

Dr. Toast is a toaster retail shop which was established to cater the toast-loving people. We provide toaster from various types and sizes of with very reasonable prices that no other shop can beat.

Our Story

Dr. Toast was established in 1996 by Corey Taylor as a retail shop for toasters, and is still standing tall today. Originally opened in a small garage by Mr. Taylor, we are now one of the biggest toaster sellers in Canada.
